What is “Pay AI” in card systems?

Pay AI is the use of machine learning and data models across the entire card lifecycle: from who gets approved, to which transactions are blocked, to which offers you see in your app.

  • Credit risk models decide who is approved
  • Real-time fraud engines score every transaction
  • AI-driven limits adapt to your behaviour
  • Personalised rewards and offers per customer
  • Collections and support flows are optimised by models

Where AI lives in the card stack

Onboarding & KYC

Identity checks, document reading and risk flags at signup.

Risk & underwriting

Models predict default probability and set starting limits.

Fraud scoring

Every transaction is scored in milliseconds for fraud risk.

Authorisation strategy

AI helps decide when to approve, challenge or decline borderline cases.

Offers & rewards

Segment-based offers, merchant-specific deals, personalised boosts.

Collections & retention

Predict who needs reminders, plans or proactive outreach.

Ops Forecasting & simulation Smarter limits, buffers and capacity planning.

Benefits – and risks – of AI in payments

AI can make payments safer and smoother, but also needs guardrails:

  • Fewer false declines on legitimate travel or large purchases
  • Faster detection of stolen cards and account takeover
  • Smarter line management instead of blunt credit cuts
  • Risk of bias if training data is not controlled
  • Need for explainability and regulatory oversight
